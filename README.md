# Dobify

We build AI systems and software for businesses in Poland, the UK and the
rest of Europe: AI chatbots and Voice AI, custom AI agents, web and mobile
apps, live dashboards, and business automations on n8n and Make.

Numbers we stand behind:

- 200+ custom automations built
- 70,000+ leads handled by our AI systems
- First-contact time at one client (Edu4u) cut from 2 hours to 30 seconds

Everything we ship is production software. The systems in our
[case studies](https://dobify.dev/en/case-studies/) run live today.

## What lives here

Previews of n8n workflows we build and give away: each repo carries a
